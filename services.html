Our Services

We offer comprehensive internal network penetration testing designed to reveal the attack paths that matter most.

Features Overview

  • High-level network and asset mapping to identify live hosts, routing boundaries and critical systems.
  • Segmentation and trust boundary analysis to reveal unintended lateral access.
  • Identity and privilege review (e.g., Active Directory) to discover over-privileged accounts and risky configurations.
  • Vulnerability assessment with a focus on exploitability, not just scanner output.
  • Controlled exploitation to safely validate critical issues and show realistic impact.
  • Blast radius assessment through lateral movement simulation and escalation checks.
  • Impact demonstration with minimal data exposure and strong documentation.
  • Detection and response evaluation to highlight monitoring gaps and opportunities for improvement.
  • Cleanup and retesting to ensure issues are resolved.

Our Procedure

Our penetration testing procedure follows a rigorous, structured approach:

  1. Authorization & Scope Lock: We begin only after written approval and clearly define what’s in and out of scope.
  2. Rules of Engagement: We agree on testing windows, rate limits, proof requirements and escalation contacts.
  3. Access & Test Positioning: We confirm where we’re plugged in—user VLAN, server segment, VPN or Wi‑Fi—and what credentials (if any) we’re given.
  4. Environment & Safety Prep: We prepare logging, time synchronisation, note‑taking and cleanup plans.
  5. High‑Level Network Mapping: We identify subnets, routing, DNS/DHCP, core services and where the crown jewels live.
  6. Asset Discovery & Service Inventory: We build a list of live hosts and key services to guide our testing.
  7. Segmentation & Trust Boundary Checks: We test how easily a workstation can talk to sensitive servers or cross network boundaries.
  8. Identity & Directory Assessment: We review authentication flows, privileged group sprawl and risky defaults to identify realistic attack paths.
  9. Credential & Access Validation: With approval, we validate weak access paths such as overly broad local admin rights, stale accounts and exposed secrets.
  10. Vulnerability Assessment Focused on Exploitability: We prioritise issues that enable access, privilege escalation, movement or data exposure.
  11. Privilege Escalation Checks: We validate whether a standard user can become local admin or gain domain‑level influence.
  12. Lateral Movement Simulation: When allowed, we simulate how an attacker could move deeper into your network.
  13. Sensitive Data Access Validation: We demonstrate impact by showing minimal, non‑sensitive evidence of access.
  14. Detection & Response Observations: We record what triggered alerts (or not) to help improve your monitoring.
  15. Cleanup: We remove any test accounts, keys or temporary configurations.
  16. Report & Debrief: We deliver clear attack path narratives, findings and prioritized fixes.
  17. Retest & Closure: We verify that fixes reduce risk and close out the test.
